# 🛒 Retail Store Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail store sales data to understand customer behavior, sales trends, product performance, and business opportunities.

Using Python and data visualization, the project provides insights to improve sales, inventory management, customer satisfaction, and store operations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze daily and hourly sales trends
- Identify peak shopping hours
- Find best-selling products and categories
- Understand customer ratings and payment preferences
- Generate business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** `DATASET_RETAIL.csv`

Contains 1380 retail transactions with details such as:

- Date & Time
- Product & Category
- Quantity
- Bill Amount
- Payment Mode
- Customer Type
- Discount
- Rating

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

### Sales Analysis
- Total Revenue: **₹341,275**
- Average Bill Amount: **₹247.30**
- Highest Sales Day: **Sunday (₹73,330)**
- Lowest Sales Day: **Monday (₹30,375)**

### Customer & Product Insights
- Peak Shopping Time: **Afternoon (2 PM - 5 PM)**
- Best Selling Category: **Grocery**
- Best Selling Product: **Rice**
- Average Customer Rating: **4.23/5**

### Customer Segmentation

Customers were grouped into:
- Low Spender
- Medium Spender
- High Spender
- Premium Customer

## 💡 Business Recommendations
